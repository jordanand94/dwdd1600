Film Summaries

  • Psycho (1960)

See Alfred Hitchcock's class film on the big screen, invoking the same chilling feeling audiences first received in 1960! Norman Bates runs Bates Motel and Marion Crane is about to pay a visit, but little does she know that Norman is hiding a dark secret. Coming soon to Cinephile!

  • Spider-Man (2002)

A film that set forth a change in film history forever in regard to the superhero genre, Sam Raimi's ambitious movie is coming soon to Cinephile. Featuring special effects that were the first of its kind, this portroyal of Spider-Man, how he became the household name we all know and love, and his romantic development with Mary Jane Watson elicits a wave of nostalgia for all fans alike.

  • Moneyball (2011)

A masterwork from screenwriter Aaron Sorkin, this movie portays general manager of the Oakland Athletics Billy Beane in the early 2000s and his unorthodox ways of developing a competitive baseball team, one that paved the way for how teams are developed today. Brad Pitt stars as Beane, alongside Jonah Hill as his analytics partner in the film. Come see one of the most praised sports films ever at Cinephile!

  • Before Sunrise (1995)

Tells the one day encounter between Jesse, an American, and Celine, a French woman, in Vienna. Although only having just met, they have conversations and experiences throughout the period of just one day that will shape their lives forever. A noteable film that began a trilogy from director Richard Linklater, come revisit this timeless film coming soon!

  • High Fidelity (2000)

After having gone through yet another breakup, a record store owner played by John Cusack retraces his steps of every other relationship he has been through to see if he really was at fault for what didn't last, or if it's the other way around. A crowd-pleasing romnatic comedy, you'll realize this is one of the best in the genre!

  • Jaws (1975)

Easily one of the most influential and popular films ever made, see this film on the big screen as it approaches its 50th anniversary! The tale of Sheriff Brodie and his hunt for the killing shark stands the test of time, and we will celebrate it here at Cinephile!
